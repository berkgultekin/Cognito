ABOUT US

The name comes from the Latin meaning of cognitive and to know. The fragmented structure of C in our logo symbols our approach from the piece to the whole; the triangular parts represents the three main aspects of this corporate: science, research and technology.

The slogan SIC PARVIS MAGNA means greatness is created with small beginnings

The vision is the combination of six important values. We believe that these values are the keys to reach the best and our solutions blended with our knowledge, technology and vision will fill the gap in the field and offer us a pleasant adventure.

Mission

To develop lean and high-quality software solutions for national and global complex problems; using domain knowledge and cutting-edge technologies

Vision

  • Partnership Perspective
    • Unite a distributed network of customers, suppliers and partners; together we create value and solve complex problems.
  • People Orientedness
    • Be a great place to work where people are inspired to do their best.
  • Holism
    • Be an easily workable, integratable software solutions-developing partner that approaches the problem holistically with domain knowledge.
  • High Quality
    • Be and be recognized as a very high-quality software company.
  • Intelligence
    • Empower intelligent algorithms in software to support users with decision-making, predicting and planning whenever possible.
  • Agility
    • Embrace agile manifesto and agile values.
